Lorde - Melodrama

Rating: 10/10 | Release Year: 2017 | Genre: Art Pop

Favourite Songs:

  • Green Light
  • Sober
  • Homemade Dynamite
  • Supercut
  • Perfect Places

Least Liked Song: Writer in the Dark

A concept album built around a relationship breaking to pieces, and my personal favorite album. It's difficult to describe how perfect this album is, combining Lorde's incredible vocals, imagery, and powerful storytelling.

Why I Recommend It: The emotional connection, the impactful lyrics, and the journey it takes you on make this a masterpiece.
