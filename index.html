Lorde - Melodrama

Listen on Spotify

Listen here...

A concept album built around a relationship breaking to pieces, and my personal favorite album. It's difficult to describe how perfect this album is, combining Lorde's incredible vocals, her imagery, and powerful storytelling.

Pink Floyd - The Dark Side of the Moon (2011 Remastered)

Listen on Spotify

Listen here...

A perfect photo of the pointless angst that humans face. The nothingness is dark, and this album explores that depth, with a common theme being the unstoppable movement of time and the struggle for purpose.

Kendrick Lamar - To Pimp A Butterfly

Listen on Spotify

Listen here...

Kendrick's magnum opus, exploring race, the music industry, and Kendrick's mind in a beautiful fluid journey. The artistic lyricism and wild jazz production make this album timeless.

my bloody valentine - loveless

A perfectly produced album that is cohesively fitting yet diverse, 'Loveless' stands out as one of the most unique and timeless records in the shoegaze genre.

Bo Burnham - Inside (The Songs)

Listen on Spotify

Listen here...

Bo Burnham's genius comedic soundtrack from his Netflix special captures the essence of 2021 with meta-humor and introspective reflections on our world, producing a compelling, near-perfect record.
